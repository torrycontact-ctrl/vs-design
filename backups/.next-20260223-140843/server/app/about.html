How I Work

Every project is different, but my process gives it structure. I adapt these steps to fit your timeline, budget, and stage — whether you need the full journey or a focused sprint.

01

Research

I dig into your product, market, and users. Competitor review, user context, analytics — whatever it takes to understand the real problem before designing anything.

02

Discovery

We align on direction together. I explore ideas, gather references, and run through early concepts so we’re building on a shared vision from day one.

03

User Experience

I map out the logic: user flows, information architecture, wireframes. This is where messy ideas become a clear, testable structure.

04

User Interface

Structure becomes a real product. Polished screens, a coherent design system, interactive prototypes — everything ready for your dev team to build.

05

Delivery & Support

I hand off developer-ready files and stay involved through implementation. Testing, iteration, and refinement until it’s right.
